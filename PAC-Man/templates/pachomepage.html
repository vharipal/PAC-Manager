PAC-Manager: Where Security Meets Simplicity

PAC-Manager generates and securely stores strong passwords, eliminating the need for you to remember them. Protect yourself against vulnerabilities caused by weak passwords.

About Us

PAC-Manager's primary goals is to facilitate the seamless importation of passwords from various platforms into your current application. This feature empowers users to maintain unique passwords for each of their websites and applications, thereby enhancing their online security. Additionally, PAC-Manager offers on-demand password generation coupled with robust strength assessment capabilities to ensure the creation of secure passwords. In the event that PAC-Manager identifies a password as potentially weak, it provides tailored recommendations to fortify its strength, further safeguarding user accounts.
